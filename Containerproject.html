About Mobile legends

A virtual game

Mobile legends is a popular mobile games based on fiction. It was made on indonessia 9 years ago. In this game, every hero has it's own role and powers to dominate the match. There are different skins of your favourite heroes which increases your gaming experience. You won't be alone there. Most interesting thing is that you can play with your friends online, communicate with your own Uid. and many more.


Opportunities are the most great thing about this game. If you became a good gamer just by playing your role. You can get jobs from different clubs and many esports tournaments are organised in singapore, indonessia and even in your own local area. You cannot miss chances like that.
